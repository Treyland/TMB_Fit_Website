Consultation Call - 2 Month Program

About this Program:

Shortly upon purchase, a trainer will reach out to you in order to schedule a consultation call. During this call, the trainer will discuss a variety of things with you so that they can get an in depth understanding of your current picture of health! They will also help you to flesh out some specific, attainable goals! Examples of things discussed include: health/workout history, goal discussions, explanation of important fitness concepts, life circumstances pertaining to fitness, answering any questions you may have! Following the call, the trainer will write you a custom made 2 month training program that is tailored specifically to your needs and goals that were discussed during the consultation call. This program is perfect for anyone (new to working out, long-time exercisers, and anyone in between) that would like personalized assistance to help them attain their goals! This is your journey and it would be an honor to help you get to where you want to be!
