MISSION

Our mission at TMB Fit is to positively impact the lives of others through fitness and to assist people in taking more confident ownership of their journey toward a healthier lifestyle.

Values

  • Client Well-Being
    • Regardless of your goals, we strive to provide you with an experience that you can take ownership of
    • We want to help you to gain the confidence to take control of your fitness and health and enjoy the process, despite the hard work
  • Decency
    • We strive to be a business that operates on a people-first basis. We intend to approach every situation with respect for everyone involved and intend to maintain a business of high character individuals.
  • Proper Movement Mechanics
    • Lead to optimal muscle function, efficient strength gains, and a healthier body
  • Eccentric Isometrics
    • Slow controlled tempo during eccentric phase (3-4 sec) followed by isometric hold (1-2 sec)
    • Optimizes kinesthetic awareness and stability as you are required to control every aspect of the Movement

Frequent Concerns

  • "I don't feel comfortable going to the gym."
    • Our service offers a variety of programs that can function in any environment.  ​We are flexible and can program for a gym environment with full equipment, a home environment with no equipment, and everything in between.
  • "I'm nervous about working with a fitness professional."
    • We pride ourselves on providing our clients with an experience that they can enjoy.  While our trainers will challenge you, their primary functions are to provide expertise and to serve as a motivational tool.  You will not be judged by anyone; we want you to feel secure in working to the best of your ability! Don't be afraid to fail!
  • "I already know what I am doing, what value can a trainer bring me?"
    • As with anything in life, there is always room for growth.  It may be the case that you've been working out for years and have hit a plateau that you can't seem to conquer. Maybe you are just looking to switch up your normal routine. Working with a trainer may give you that extra boost that you need.  Again, our mission is to help you take ownership of your fitness journey, so we can also serve as a resource to bounce ideas off of if you are already confident in your fitness.
